A Note On Age Categories

 

For 2015

For the 2015 race, the categories for runners and teams are consistent with those used introduced on the 2013 race. The categories used are: Mens Open, Mens Masters (35-44, 45-54 and 55+); similarly for the ladies: Womens Open, Womens Masters (35-44, 45-54 and 55+). Team prizes (one each for Men and for Women) are also still available.

Historically

  1. The Male: 16-39 category operated between 2004 and 2007. It was absorbed into the new Male: Open category for the 2008 race and for subsequent races.
  2. The Female: 16-34 category operated between 2004 and 2007. It was absorbed into the new Female: Open category for the 2008 race and for subsequent races.
  3. The Male: 40 and Over category operated for 2004 only and was then absorbed into the new Male: 40-49 category for subsequent races.
  4. The Female: 35 and Over category operated for 2004 only and was then absorbed into the new Female: 35-44 category for subsequent races.
  5. The Male: Open and Female: Open categories were introduced in 2008.
  6. The Male and Female Team prize categories were introduced in 2011. These team prizes are also noted in the winners summary table from 2011.
  7. Categories were once again re-defined from 2013 as follows:-
    • Mens Open (first 3)
    • Mens Masters
      • Men 35-44
      • Men 45-54
      • Men 55+
    • Mens Team Prize (for pre-registered teams of 4)
    • Ladies Open (first 3)
    • Ladies Masters
      • Women 35-44
      • Women 45-54
      • Women 55+
    • Womens Team Prize (for pre-registered teams of 3)
